Apple iPhone 17 Pro Design & Build Quality

The iPhone 17 Pro measures 149.6 × 71.5 × 8.3 mm and weighs 199g, making it comfortable to hold while still feeling durable. The body uses reinforced Corning glass on the front and back, while the frame is made of Grade-5 titanium for strength and a premium look. Its 6.3-inch display covers nearly 90% of the front, delivering a cinematic viewing experience with a 19.5:9 ratio.

Operating System & Processor

The device ships with iOS 26 featuring enhanced security, smarter Siri AI capabilities, and deeper integration with Apple devices. Powered by the 3nm Apple A19 Pro six-core processor and a six-core Apple GPU, performance remains extremely smooth in apps, gaming, and heavy workloads. With 12GB RAM and 256GB NVMe storage, multitasking and storage feel effortless.

Camera System

Apple once again raises the mobile photography standard. The triple-camera system includes a 48MP wide camera, 48MP ultra-wide camera, and 48MP periscope telephoto lens, enabling detailed shots and 5× optical zoom. Video features include 4K recording up to 120fps, Dolby Vision HDR, Cinematic Mode, and spatial video capture. A 12MP front camera with SL-3D depth sensor ensures secure Face ID and high-quality selfies.
